The purpose of the global website is to cater to users who will access the website from outside India. The content and structure of the global website will be similar to the Indian vertical, but with a focus on showcasing our services and expertise to an international audience. The global website will highlight our ability to provide advisory and services to businesses worldwide, emphasizing our network of certified professionals and our commitment to helping businesses grow and succeed globally. The global website will also include information about our experience working with international clients, our understanding of global business regulations, and our ability to provide tailored solutions for businesses operating in different countries. The goal is to position Snowfall Advisory as a trusted partner for businesses around the world, offering comprehensive advisory services that enable them to focus on their core operations while we take care of their compliance and growth needs. It will also include a clear call-to-action for international businesses to get in touch with us for a strategic growth session, showcasing our commitment to helping businesses succeed on a global scale. We plan on facilitiating every requirement that would be needed for a business to operate in India from outside India, be it compliance, legal, tax or funding related. We will also have a separate section for global businesses which will highlight the services we provide to them and how we can help them expand their operations in India. The global website will be designed to be user-friendly and informative, providing valuable insights and resources for international businesses looking to enter the Indian market or seeking advisory services for their global operations.
